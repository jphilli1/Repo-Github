Normalization Methodology:

  • Excluded Segments: Domestic C&I, NDFI (Fund Finance), ADC (Construction), Credit Cards, Auto Loans, Ag Loans
  • Purpose: Creates "apples-to-apples" comparison by removing Mass Market Consumer and Commercial Banking segments that MSPBNA does not participate in

  • Norm Rates: Numerator exclusions (NCOs, Nonaccruals) divided by Norm Gross Loans
